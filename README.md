# LearnGraph

AI-powered learning platform that transforms any content into a personalized, retention-optimized learning experience.

---

## Features

**Upload Anything, Learn Everything**
Import PDFs, YouTube videos, audio recordings, slides, Word docs, and images. LearnGraph turns any content into a structured learning experience automatically.

**AI-Generated Summaries**
Get instant TL;DRs, detailed breakdowns, and expert-level overviews of your uploaded content — no reading required to get the big picture.

**AI Mentor (Not a Chatbot)**
An AI tutor that teaches using the Socratic method — asking guiding questions, adapting explanations to your level, and scaffolding your understanding step by step.

**Smart Quizzes & Flashcards**
Auto-generated multiple choice, short-answer, and explain-back questions. No manual card creation — just study.

**Spaced Repetition Reviews**
A daily review queue powered by forgetting-curve science (FSRS). LearnGraph tells you exactly what to review today so nothing fades from memory.

**Your Personal Knowledge Graph**
See all the concepts you've learned as a visual map. Track your mastery level per topic — what you know, what's decaying, and what you've never been tested on.

**Knowledge Gap Detection** _(Beta)_
Set a learning goal or target exam — LearnGraph highlights exactly what you're missing and builds a targeted study plan to close the gap.

**Curriculum Builder** _(Beta)_
Tell LearnGraph what you want to learn. It generates a sequenced, step-by-step curriculum sourced from your own uploads and the web.

**Explain-Back Mode** _(Beta)_
Teach the concept back to the AI. It evaluates your understanding, catches misconceptions, and gives corrective feedback — the most effective learning method known to science.
